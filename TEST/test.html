我的台灣回憶

Taipei 101: A Towering Experience

Standing at the foot of Taipei 101, I was awestruck by its imposing height and unique design. The elevator ride to the observation deck was an experience in itself, and the panoramic view of Taipei city from the top was breathtaking. I remember how the setting sun painted the sky in vibrant hues, casting a golden glow over the bustling cityscape below.

Shilin Night Market: A Feast for the Senses

The vibrant atmosphere of Shilin Night Market is something I'll never forget. The aroma of stinky tofu and grilled squid filled the air as I wandered through the maze-like alleys. I tried my hand at various carnival games and indulged in an array of street foods. The lively chatter of vendors and visitors created a symphony of urban life that encapsulated the essence of Taiwanese night markets.

Taroko Gorge: Nature's Masterpiece

Hiking through Taroko Gorge was like stepping into another world. The sheer marble cliffs, crystalline waters of the Liwu River, and lush vegetation created a landscape of unparalleled beauty. I remember the exhilaration of crossing suspension bridges and exploring hidden shrines nestled within the gorge. The tranquility of this natural wonder offered a stark contrast to the bustling cities, showcasing Taiwan's diverse landscapes.
